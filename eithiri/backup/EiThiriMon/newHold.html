How to hold during an Earthquake

Step 3

HOLD: stay put and hold on until shaking stops

Grip your shelter with one hand and keep the other protecting your head/neck. Be ready to move with the shelter if it shifts.

Aftershocks can follow
1

Hold your shelter

Grip a table leg or the desk frame. If you're beside an interior wall, keep your forearm braced and head covered.

2

Stay until it's over

Do not run outside during shaking. Remain until the movement stops and it is safe to move.

3

Expect aftershocks

After the main shaking, pause, check for hazards, then evacuate if necessary. Be ready to Drop, Cover, and Hold again.
