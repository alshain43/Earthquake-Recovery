Your essential steps to stay safe during and after an earthquake.

During an Earthquake

Drop

Get low on your hands and knees to prevent being knocked over.

Cover

Take shelter under sturdy furniture and protect your head and neck.

Hold On

Hold on to your shelter until the shaking stops.

Stay Calm

Keep calm and follow your safety plan without rushing.

After an Earthquake

Check for Injuries & PTSD

Seek medical attention if injured. Get emotional support to manage trauma.

Turn Off Utilities

Shut off gas, water, and electricity if there is damage.

Stay Away from Hazards

Keep clear of broken glass, debris, and unstable buildings.
