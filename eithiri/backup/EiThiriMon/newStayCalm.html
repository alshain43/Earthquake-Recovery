Stay Calm During an Earthquake

Remaining calm helps you think clearly and follow safety steps. Panic can cause accidents and make the situation worse. Focus on protecting yourself and those around you.

1

Breathe steadily

Take slow, deep breaths to stay focused. This reduces fear and helps your brain think clearly.

2

Remember the plan

Think of the safety steps: Drop, Cover, Hold. Following them step by step keeps you safe.

3

Reassure others

Encourage classmates, friends, or family to stay calm. A calm voice can stop panic and keep order.

Tip: Practice earthquake drills regularly. The more you practice, the easier it is to stay calm in a real earthquake.
