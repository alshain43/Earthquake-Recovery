How to Cover during an Earthquake

Step 2

COVER: shield your head and go under something sturdy

Use your arms and a sturdy object (desk, table, or interior wall) to protect yourself from falling debris and glass.

Windows = danger zone
1

Use what's close

Table or desk? Slide under. No furniture? Stay next to an interior wall, away from windows and hanging objects.

2

Protect from glass

Turn your face away from windows. Keep your forearm over your head and your other arm covering your neck.

3

Backpack/pillow shield

If handy, pull a backpack, cushion, or book over your head/neck as extra shielding.
