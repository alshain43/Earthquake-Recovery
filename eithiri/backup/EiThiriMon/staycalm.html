Stay Calm During Earthquake

What To Do:

1. Drop, Cover, and Hold On

Get down low, take cover under a sturdy table or desk, and hold on until the shaking stops.

2. Stay Away from Windows

Move away from glass, mirrors, and anything that could shatter and cause injury.

3. Stay Indoors Until It's Safe

Do not run outside during the shaking. Wait until the shaking stops and follow safety instructions.
